Top three activities to do in Manchester

Christmas Markets

With over 220 stalls across nine sites throughout the city centre, this year's Christmas Markets bring some much-needed festive cheer.

Afflecks Palace

A cornerstone of alternative culture and Mancunian spirit. Find over 100 small and independent businesses with weird and wonderful wares.

Visit the Museums

Experience art and culture - from The Whitworth, our local gallery in the park, to the Museum of Science and Industry.
